### What is Saddar?

Saddar is Pakistan's leading wholesale e-commerce platform that connects retailers and businesses with verified suppliers. Named after the famous Saddar Bazaar in Karachi (the traditional wholesale hub), the platform brings the entire Saddar market experience online.

**Mission:** To digitize Pakistan's wholesale business landscape by providing retailers access to quality products at wholesale prices without traveling to physical markets.

**Target Audience:**
- Retail shop owners
- Small business owners
- E-commerce sellers (reselling on Daraz, OLX, Instagram, Facebook)
- Bulk buyers for personal use

- Meaning: Credit/debt tracking book (khata)
