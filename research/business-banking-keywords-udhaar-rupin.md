## Executive Summary

Rupin (formerly Udhaar Book) has obtained an EMI (Electronic Money Institution) license from the State Bank of Pakistan — unlocking a new high-intent keyword category: **business banking**. Users searching for "business account," "merchant wallet," "digital business banking Pakistan," and related terms are high-LTV targets who need both payments AND business management. This report identifies, clusters, and prioritizes the full set of business banking keywords to capture this audience.

**Top Opportunity:**
The gap between Rupin's current "khata/bookkeeping" keyword coverage and the "business banking / merchant wallet" keyword space is massive. JazzCash Business (7.6M downloads), NayaPay, and SadaPay all rank for these high-intent terms. Rupin's integrated wallet + business management offering makes it uniquely positioned to win these keywords.
