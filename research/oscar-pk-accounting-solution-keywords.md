## 1. Page Context Summary

Oscar.pk's accounting solution page positions Oscar POS as a business accounting tool that goes beyond point-of-sale. The page highlights:

- **Automated bookkeeping** - eliminates manual ledger work
- **Expense tracking** - monitor spending per vendor and category
- **Financial reports** - profit & loss, balance sheet, income statement, cash flow
- **Customizable accounting reports** - revenue, expenses, and profit margins in real-time
- **Cloud-based access** - view financial data from anywhere, anytime (FBR POS cloud)
- **Sales details** - itemized, by customer, and by representative (SRB POS)
- **Invoice generation** - branded invoices with logos and automatic payment notifications
- **Dashboard monitoring** - business performance at a glance with graphs
- **Reduced paperwork & errors** - digitized accounting with ready-to-use templates
- **FBR/SRB tax compliance** - GST-compliant invoices, automatic tax calculations, return filing
