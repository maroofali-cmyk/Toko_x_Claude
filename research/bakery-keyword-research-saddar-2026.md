## Executive Summary

This report provides a comprehensive keyword research framework for Saddar.com.pk's expansion into the bakery supplies and equipment category. Pakistan's bakery market is growing rapidly — home bakers, small bakery owners, caterers, and confectionery businesses all represent active buyer segments actively searching online for wholesale equipment, supplies, and ingredients.

The competitive landscape is fragmented: Bakers Supplies (bakerssupplies.pk), Bakeware.pk, BakingItems.com, Bake Away (bakeawaypk.com), Selectie.pk, and Daraz.pk are the primary online competitors. None of them have Saddar's B2B wholesale positioning, verified supplier network, or integration with Udhaar Book's 1.4M+ registered businesses. This creates significant hijacking opportunity across the entire keyword landscape.
