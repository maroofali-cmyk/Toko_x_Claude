### Best Practices & Frameworks

1. **The SADDAR Tweet Framework**
   - **S**tatistic: Lead with a compelling number or data point
   - **A**ttention: Hook with a bold statement about business/profit
   - **D**emonstrate: Show real value or success story
   - **D**iscuss: Ask questions or invite engagement
   - **A**ction: Include clear CTA (register, visit, learn more)
   - **R**epeat: Consistent posting schedule

2. **The 4-2-1 Engagement Rule for Saddar**
   - 4 value-adding tweets (business tips, market insights)
   - 2 engagement tweets (reply, quote tweet, polls)
   - 1 promotional tweet (product, discount, feature)

3. **The Thread Architecture for Success Stories**
   - Hook: "How [Retailer Name] from [City] makes Rs. X monthly with Saddar"
   - Build: Background, challenges, discovery of Saddar
   - Journey: First order, scaling up, current business
   - Results: Specific numbers, profit margins
   - CTA: How others can start their journey

4. **The Pakistani Business Hours Model**
   - Morning (9-11 AM): Business tips, motivational content
   - Afternoon (2-4 PM): Product showcases, deals
   - Evening (7-9 PM): Success stories, engagement tweets
   - Late night (10 PM-12 AM): Casual business banter
